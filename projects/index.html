Projects

Below are a few projects that I’m currently working on.

R Speaks Non Linear Mixed Effects Modeling (RsNLME)

R Speaks Non Linear Mixed Effects Modeling, RsNLME, is a suite of R packages and supplementary Shiny apps supporting pharmacometric workflows inside R.

Certara.R

A hub for R development at Certara. Certara.R provides installation of R packages developed by Certara from non-standard repositories. Certara R School and various learnr tutorials can also be accessed from this website.

R School

Certara R School is a series of guided webinars, exercises, and interactive tutorials for the application of R in Pharmacometrics.

Neuro Fungible Tokens

“Neuro-Fungible Tokens” explores the relationship between brain data and digital art. In essence, how MRI provides a lens for understanding internal brain states and the structural aesthetics from which these functions follow.

I’ve developed the neurogen package as a mechanism to create Neuro-Fungible-Tokens. The neurogen package was born from a collaboration between myself and brothers Daniel and Tyler Wishard, UCLA for a proposal at the 2022 Organization of Human Brain Mapping in Glasgow, Scotland.

Proceeds from the sale of any NFT’s will be donated to organizations conducting neurological disease research.
